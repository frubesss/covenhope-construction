About

Building company Covenhope Construction was founded in 2000 by Ian Hamilton, a builder with over 25 years experience and a commitment to the highest standards of reliability and craftsmanship.

The company carries out all types of building work, including new construction, renovations and extensions and has particular expertise in restoring and enhancing historic buildings, including timber framing. Based in Herefordshire, Covenhope Construction can also undertake work in the Welsh Borders, Shropshire, Powys and Worcestershire.
